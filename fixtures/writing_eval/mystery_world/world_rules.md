# World Rules

## Detective Agency Rules
- All case files must remain confidential; unauthorized disclosure to third parties is prohibited.
- Evidence obtained without proper chain-of-custody documentation is inadmissible in formal investigation.
- A suspect interrogation requires at least two officers present; solo interrogation yields invalid testimony.

## Evidence Hierarchy
- Physical evidence outranks documentary evidence.
- Documentary evidence outranks witness testimony.
- Speculation alone cannot override any higher-tier evidence.

## Orphanage Fire Rules
- The 1998 orphanage fire official record states seven children died, but the actual death toll was concealed.
- Any claim that the official record is fully accurate is a forbidden assertion.
- The burn scars on victims' wrists originated from the orphanage fire metal doorknob.
- Any claim that the scars are unrelated to the fire is a forbidden assertion.

## Suspect Relations
- Interrogating officers must maintain procedural neutrality toward all suspects.
- Personal history with a suspect must be disclosed before participation in the interrogation.
